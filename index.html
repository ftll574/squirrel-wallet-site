隱私是機制，不是 slogan

圖片送出去之前，
條碼先在你的裝置打碼。

三句話講完整個機制，沒有星號小字。

1

在裝置先打碼

本機 ML 模型偵測並用黑塊覆蓋所有條碼 / QR 區域。失敗就中止上傳，不會降級送原圖。

2

只上傳遮罩過的圖

送到 OpenAI 的圖已經沒有條碼。即使供應商保留圖片也無法兌換你的票券（OpenAI 預設 30 天保留、不訓練）。

3

本機 Hive + AES 加密

票券資料 AES-256 加密存在你手機上。沒有帳號、沒有雲端、沒有 analytics。截圖在 App 私有目錄，OS 已經隔離。

Privacy as mechanism, not slogan

Before any image leaves your device,
the barcode goes black.

Three sentences describe the whole flow. No footnotes.

1

On-device masking

A local ML model detects and blacks out every barcode / QR region. If detection fails, the upload aborts — there's no fallback to the unmasked original.

2

Only the masked image uploads

OpenAI receives an image with no barcodes. Default API retention is 30 days, not used for training, and our policy doesn't expand that.

3

Local Hive + AES-256

Tickets are AES-256 encrypted on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no analytics. Screenshots live in the app-private directory.

Read the full mechanism — what gets sent, what doesn't — in our Privacy Policy.
