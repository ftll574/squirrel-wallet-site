隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 5 月 4 日

感謝您使用 Squirrel Wallet（以下簡稱「本 App」）。我們重視您的隱私權，本政策說明本 App 如何處理您的資料。

1. 我們不收集您的個人資料

本 App 沒有帳號系統，不要求您註冊、登入或提供姓名、電話、電子郵件、生日、地理位置等個人識別資料。我們也不使用任何第三方 analytics（如 Google Analytics、Firebase Analytics），因此沒有任何使用者行為追蹤。

2. 您的資料儲存在您的裝置上

您透過本 App 新增的所有票券、優惠券、條碼、截圖、eSIM 安裝資訊，皆儲存於您裝置的本地資料庫（Hive）與系統偏好（SharedPreferences），不會上傳到任何雲端伺服器。當您刪除 App，這些資料就會一併移除。

3. AI 影像辨識使用第三方服務

當您使用「AI 辨識」功能時，本 App 會將您拍攝或選取的圖片傳送至以下第三方視覺語言模型 API 進行辨識：

  • OpenRouter
  • Google Gemini API
  • Groq

傳送內容僅包含您主動選擇辨識的圖片，不附帶任何使用者識別資訊（無 device ID、無使用者帳號、無位置資料）。請參考各服務商的隱私權政策了解其資料處理方式。如果您不希望使用此功能，請改用手動輸入或條碼掃描。

4. 系統權限的用途

  • 相機（Camera）：用於掃描票券條碼以及拍攝票券圖片進行 AI 辨識。
  • 相簿（Photo Library）：用於選擇現有圖片進行 AI 辨識。本 App 僅讀取您主動選取的單張圖片，不會掃描您整個相簿。
  • 通知（Notifications）：用於票券到期前的本地提醒。所有通知都由您的裝置在本機產生，不會發送任何使用者資料到伺服器。

5. 資料分享

除前述 AI 辨識服務的圖片傳送之外，本 App 不會將您的任何資料分享給任何第三方。

未來版本（v2）可能加入「使用者主動分享優惠券資訊」之社群功能，屆時將為 opt-in 設計，使用者必須明確開啟才會啟用，相關隱私變動將更新本政策並另行通知。

6. 兒童隱私

本 App 適用於 13 歲以上使用者。若您未滿 13 歲，請由監護人協助使用。我們不會刻意收集 13 歲以下兒童的資料（事實上我們不收集任何使用者資料）。

7. 資料安全

本 App 對外連線（AI 辨識、後續 v2 同步功能）皆使用 HTTPS 加密。本機儲存的資料受 iOS / Android 系統沙盒機制保護；如您將備份檔案匯出，該檔案以您指定的位置儲存，請您自行妥善保管。

8. 政策變更

若本政策有實質變更，我們會於本頁更新「最後更新」日期，並於 App 內顯示通知。重大變更（如新增資料收集行為）將於變更生效前 30 天告知。

9. 聯絡我們

如有任何隱私權相關問題或要求，請來信：support@squirrelwallet.app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Thank you for using Squirrel Wallet (the "App"). We respect your privacy. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

1. We do not collect your personal data

The App has no account system. We do not ask you to register, sign in, or provide your name, phone number, email, date of birth, or location. We also do not use any third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Firebase Analytics, no anything else), so no user behavior is tracked.

2. Your data lives on your device

All tickets, coupons, barcodes, screenshots, and eSIM info you add to the App are stored locally on your device using Hive and SharedPreferences. Nothing is uploaded to any cloud server. When you uninstall the App, this data is removed with it.

3. AI image recognition uses third-party services

When you use the "AI recognition" feature, the App sends the image you've photographed or selected to one of the following third-party Vision Language Model APIs:

  • OpenRouter
  • Google Gemini API
  • Groq

The transmission contains only the image you explicitly chose to recognize, with no user-identifying metadata attached (no device ID, no user account, no location). Please consult each provider's privacy policy for how they handle data. If you'd rather not use this feature, you can enter ticket details manually or scan the barcode instead.

4. System permissions

  • Camera: used to scan ticket barcodes and to photograph tickets for AI recognition.
  • Photo Library: used to pick an existing image for AI recognition. The App only reads the single image you explicitly select; it does not scan your full library.
  • Notifications: used to remind you before a ticket expires. All notifications are generated locally on your device — no user data is sent to any server for them.

5. Data sharing

Beyond the AI recognition image transmission described above, the App does not share any of your data with any third party.

A future version (v2) may add an opt-in feature for users to share coupon information socially. That feature will require explicit user activation; this policy will be updated and you will be notified before any related data behavior changes.

6. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. If you are under 13, please use the App with a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (in fact, we do not collect data from any user).

7. Security

All outbound connections (AI recognition, future v2 sync features) use HTTPS encryption. Local data is protected by the iOS / Android system sandbox. If you export a backup file, you are responsible for keeping that file safe at the location you chose.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and show a notice inside the App. Significant changes (e.g., new data collection) will be announced at least 30 days before they take effect.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please email support@squirrelwallet.app.
